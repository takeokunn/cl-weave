# Code of Conduct

`cl-weave` aims to be a practical, welcoming open source project. Everyone
participating in issues, pull requests, reviews, and discussions is expected to
help keep the project constructive and low-friction.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and direct.
- Assume good faith unless evidence shows otherwise.
- Focus criticism on ideas, implementations, and tradeoffs.
- Keep feedback specific, actionable, and scoped to the change.
- Respect different experience levels and working styles.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, discrimination, or hate speech.
- Deliberate trolling, personal attacks, or repeated bad-faith argument.
- Publishing private information without consent.
- Using review or discussion threads to intimidate, shame, or exclude people.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, or limit participation when
behavior violates this code of conduct. Repeated or severe violations may lead
to a permanent ban from project spaces.

If you need to report a concern, open a private issue if available or contact
the maintainers through the repository's security or contribution channels.
