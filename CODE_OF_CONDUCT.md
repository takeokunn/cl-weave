# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in the `cl-weave` community a respectful,
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size,
disability, ethnicity, gender identity or expression, level of experience,
nationality, personal appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and
orientation.

## Expected Behavior

- Be respectful and constructive in technical discussion.
- Assume good intent while addressing behavior and evidence directly.
- Welcome differing viewpoints and explain decisions clearly.
- Focus feedback on code, documentation, and project decisions rather than
  people.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, discriminatory language, or personal attacks.
- Deliberate intimidation, trolling, or sustained disruption.
- Publishing private information without permission.
- Conduct that would be unreasonable in a professional open-source project.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove, reject, or moderate contributions and discussions that
violate this code. Use the repository issue tracker for reports that can be
handled publicly. For a report requiring privacy, contact the repository owner
through a contact method listed on their GitHub profile. The private advisory
route in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md) is reserved for vulnerabilities.

Enforcement decisions follow the maintainer responsibilities documented in
[docs/src/governance.md](docs/src/governance.md).
